Hey, I'm Yuvraj — the developer behind Restify. Restify started as a personal project driven by my interest in music, design, and building clean user experiences. The goal is simple: create a lightweight, smooth, and distraction-free music platform focused on listening, not clutter. I enjoy experimenting with web technologies, UI design, and new ideas that can make digital products feel faster, cleaner, and more enjoyable to use. Restify is where I test those ideas and continue learning as a developer. This project is not a public music streaming platform and is not intended for commercial distribution. Any audio content used during development, testing, or demonstrations is included solely for educational and project-development purposes. Restify is a private project created to learn, experiment, and showcase technical implementation rather than to publicly publish or distribute copyrighted music. Thanks for checking out the project and being part of its journey.
